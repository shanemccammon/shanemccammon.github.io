U.S. Army 2013 - 2021

I served in the Indiana Army National Guard for seven years, followed by one additional year in the Inactive Ready Reserve (IRR), and was honorably discharged at the rank of Sergeant (E-5). I began in a traditional drilling status and later supported multiple periods of active-duty service. Throughout my career, I had the privilege of serving in a variety of roles and learning from exceptional leaders across several units, including:

  • 2013–2014: Training
  • 2014–2015: 76th Infantry Brigade Combat Team – 113th BSB
  • 2015–2016: 81st Troop Command
  • 2016–2018: 219th Engineer Brigade – 113th Engineer Battalion
  • 2018–2019: 38th Combat Aviation Brigade – 638th ASB
  • 2019–2020: 244th Expeditionary Combat Aviation Brigade – 638th ASB (Deployment)
